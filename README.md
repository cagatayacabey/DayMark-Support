### How do I add a widget to my home screen?
1. Long press your home screen
2. Tap the **+** button in the top left corner
3. Search for **"DayMark"**
4. Choose Small, Medium, or Large
5. Tap **"Add Widget"**

### How do I create a new event?
1. Open DayMark app
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Enter event name and date
4. Choose an icon
5. Tap **Save**

### Does DayMark work offline?
Yes! All your events are stored locally on your device.

### How often do widgets update?
Widgets automatically update every hour or when you open the app.
